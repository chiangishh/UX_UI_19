Overview

Stop Asian Hate is a social movement mobile app that ncourage positivity and awareness for the Stop AAPI Hate social movement.

Role :

User Experience & Interface Design, User Research

Team :

Vivian Chiang, Gloria Albiter, Michelle Lee

Tools Used :

Figma, Miro, Photoshop, Trello

Duration :

2 weeks

- Problem

How might we provide the users with a clear information architecture to find what they need to enrolling their child in workshops or memberships?

We believe that providing the users a clear layout of information on the activities will help customers understand what they are enrolling their child in and thus increase membership subscriptions.

By providing information about the workshops/activities for parents to book for their elementary kids will result in higher at home activity download rates and membership sign ups.
